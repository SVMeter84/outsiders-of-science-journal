About Outsiders of Science

Outsiders of Science is a free, ethical, and non-gatekeeping journal founded on the belief that scientific discovery should not be restricted by credentials, paywalls, or conformity. We are a space for independent researchers, maverick theorists, and rogue thinkers to present falsifiable, testable, and bold ideas.

Our peer review system is transparent and community-led, emphasizing open commentary, reasoned debate, and the cultivation of new perspectives rather than suppression. Anyone who contributes meaningfully can participate in the review process.

We believe science should be about ideas, not institutions. Welcome to the frontier.
